Professional background

Prior to becoming a programmer, Shaunna worked extensively in the fields of language and education. In addition to being a native English speaker, she is fluent in Spanish, holding a Master of Arts degree in Spanish literature from the University of Oregon and a certificate from the Univeristy of Salamanca for "near-native" Spanish. Additionally, she lived in Italy for 4 years and is fluent in Italian.

Shaunna's career in education is expansive and diverse. She has taught Spanish as an adjunct professor at several community colleges in Oregon. She taught English to adults and children at a private English school in Rome, Italy for 4 years. She holds a Master of Science in Speech Language Pathology from the Portland State University, and worked as a bilingual Spanish-English speech language pathologist for Portland Public School district for 3 years.
